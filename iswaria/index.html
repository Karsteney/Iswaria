The main continent of our campaign is called Iswaria

There are 11 main parts of the continent, with many notable cities to explore and characters to meet.

Continents

Redwood Isles

Series of islands around a larger island with a volcano. Known for its free ports and population of pirates. Generally lawless but they do refer to a code to keep some sense of an organized chaos. Most forms of morals are accepted so long as they follow some of the rules while in the cities.
