The main continent of our campaign is called Iswaria

There are 11 main parts of the continent, with many notable cities to explore and characters to meet.

Continents

Redwood Isles

Series of islands around a larger island with a volcano. Known for its free ports and population of pirates. Generally lawless but they do refer to a code to keep some sense of an organized chaos. Most forms of morals are accepted so long as they follow some of the rules while in the cities.

Tiran Mists

An landmass of Plateaus connected by bridges with valleys full of mist. Many great tinkerers, archeologists, and explorers come from that area and rise to fame thanks to their findings down in the mist. No one currently knows how deep the mist goes, or where there missing explorers or adventurers go.

Yellowback Jungle

A dense tropical jungle filled with many strange beasts and untapped resources. There have been rare minerals and materials found in those woods, but they are normally extremely difficult to reach or are guarded by some strong creatures. Merchants would hire adventurers to obtain the resources and sell them all over the world.

The jungle reaches around a region known as the Ao Crater. The origin of this crater is relatively unknown as there are many different stories about it. However the current Era is notable named after it, with the year being 737 AA, or After Ao.

Nightshade Woods

A large, long dead forest full of various monsters of the night. For the past few hundred years, the area of the dead woods has expanded, so much so that the nearby island has started dying too. Teams of adventurers and clerics have been sent in to fight the monsters, but they have not found the reason for the spreading plague yet.

Blackstone Desert

Vast land of scorching deserts with little vegetation or shade. It’s the only connecting land between The eastern and western parts of Iswaria. The land is dangerous, filled with clans of monstrous creatures, orcs, chimeras, goliaths, and others of the sort. Not all are hostile, but traveling with a group or with a guard is advised.

Mithril Prominence

Vast mountain ranges reaching into the sky. Home to many dwarven cities and great craftsmen. Generally closed off to outsiders, through some of the lower cities and areas are open to travelers moving through the area. Moth cities operate separately and in isolation.

Duskwallow Marsh

Low marshes off the northern coast of the Mithril Prominence. Several towns litter the marshes, through relatively small and quiet. Strange disappearances have been reported over the years, mainly the travelers staying in the towns closer to the center of the marshes. Some have speculated Fey magics, some think witches.

Rift Hills

Magical lands, home to many of the new and upcoming wizards and mages. Many of the towns are relatively new, being built within the past few hundred years since the time of Ao. Mainly known for their creation of magical items and exportation to other lands. There have been rumors and occurrences of hills and parts of mountains disappearing from time to time. Sometimes returning, sometimes reappearing in new locations, and sometimes not returning at all.

Silvered Plains

Home to many of the great kingdoms of man. Known for their great calvary armies and wide reaching merchants. Several kingdoms sprawl the plains, each under the rule of different lords. The region however is exceptionally religious. Most people don't outright ask you who you worship, but worshipping the wrong gods openly can be dangerous.

Trosten Forest

Dense forests, home to many Fey, beasts, and druids. Many in the woods have learned to respect the animals and the rule of the forest, though some manage to thrive despite the dangers. Relatively peaceful lands, through hit is slowly disappearing due to the humans from the north taking the woods for their homes.

Frostcrown Peaks

Relatively untouched lands. Few live there, and those who do have been there for many generations, slowly learning how to survive and grow accustomed to the land. Some have started settling deep within the mountains to stay warm, but they inadvertently released some long frozen and forgotten creatures who now roam the peaks.
